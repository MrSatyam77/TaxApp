Welcome

  • Our Conversion Wizard tool allows you to convert your existing returns, client information, schedules and forms into MyTAXPrepOffice quickly and easily.

  • To Navigate the Conversion Wizard simply click on the Next button, or the Previous button to return to the previous screen.

  • Important Conversion Information

    1. You will need to locate your software file(s).

      1. The Conversion Wizard will provide you the default data path

    2. Upload your file(s), and wait for your conversion request to be processed.

      1. You will be able to upload one or more files

      2. If you upload more than one file, you will have the option to Pause and Resume the Upload Process.

    3. After your returns are converted, you will need to verify all returns to ensure accurate calculations.

      1. Due to differences between various tax programs, not all data from the original returns will be converted.

      2. The conversion process does not recreate the original 2018 return. Click here to review Conversion information.

      3. Various tax programs utilize many different combinations of depreciation methods. Some methods of depreciation listed in the prior software may not be able to be converted. We recommend that you verify the asset depreciation closely for accuracy.

  • Need help with your conversion?

  • If you have any questions or need help with your conversion, please contact our Customer Support Team using the live chat feature within your software or email us support@mytaxprepoffice.com.
